💊 Treatment Strategy
AI-generated antibiotic stewardship recommendations
Stewardship Principles
#1
Reserve Carbapenems
Use imipenem/meropenem only for confirmed multidrug-resistant organisms. High F1 on environmental strains indicates local carbapenem resistance is emerging.
#2
Gentamicin First-line Aminoglycoside
F1=0.711 — model confidence is high. Preferred for Gram-negative UTIs where quinolone resistance is detected.
#3
Avoid Empiric Fluoroquinolones
CIP resistance predicted in >40% of E. coli isolates from community settings. Always culture-guide when possible.
#4
Combination Therapy for ESBL Producers
blaCTX-M is the strongest predictor of multi-drug resistance. Consider beta-lactam/beta-lactamase inhibitor combinations (AMC).
#5
De-escalate After Culture Results
AI prediction guides empiric choice; always de-escalate to narrowest effective agent once susceptibility is confirmed.
